About Me

Frontend Developer

During my secondary school years, I was introduced to coding but didn't fully grasp its workings, so I didn't pursue it. However, in 2022, my uncle, also in the tech field, introduced me to HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. He revealed the possibility of creating websites with these languages and showed me the basics. Since then, my interest in learning them has surged.

I began exploring various free resources like FREECODE CAMP, BRO CODE, CODER-CODER, SIMPLILEARN, and others. Despite experiencing frustrations during my learning journey, I remained determined and committed to learning more every day.

My choice to specialize in frontend development stems from my passion for drawing, painting, and connecting with the natural world. I see frontend development as akin to my artistic pursuits: drawing for structure, painting for styling, and using JavaScript to infuse a sense of interaction for the user. This unique blend resonates with my creative inclinations and fuels my enthusiasm for frontend development.

I like to work on personal projects or any team project. This approach allows me to delve into individual explorations while also thriving in collaborative environments. In my experience, the synergy between personal creativity and collective effort often leads to innovative solutions. Whether working independently or within a team, I find immense satisfaction in translating my artistic vision into captivating user experiences.
